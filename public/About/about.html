About Us

MindMentor is a vision-driven platform created by three passionate students from Computer Science (AI & ML). Having journeyed through the same doubts, decisions, and dilemmas as you, we understand how overwhelming it can feel to choose the right career.

Our mission is simple: to guide you towards a fulfilling career path based on your strengths, interests, and personality using the power of Artificial Intelligence. We believe that every student deserves a personalized direction — not just random advice. At MindMentor, your dreams take center stage. Let’s explore, learn, and build your future together!
